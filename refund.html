Refund Policy

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available for items returned within 30 days of delivery, provided they meet our return conditions.

Refund Processing Time

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Refund Amount

The refund amount will include the product price. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Cancelled Orders

If you cancel an order before it ships, you will receive a full refund. If the order has already shipped, standard return procedures apply.

Contact Us

For refund inquiries, please contact our customer support at refunds@shophub.com
